🎯 Tanpura Practice Guide
STEP 1 — SUN KE SAMJHO
Pehle sirf tanpura suno — 2 minute. Koi sur mat nikalo. Bas feel karo.
STEP 2 — HUM KARO
"Mmmmm" ki awaaz andar hi andar nikalo. Sur ko feel karo apne seene mein.
STEP 3 — "SA" PAKDO
"Aaaaaa" bolte hue tanpura ke sur se milao. Jab dono ek jaisa lage — tab sahi hai.
